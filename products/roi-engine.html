The Problem We Solve

If You Cannot Measure Signal Impact, You Cannot Optimize Intelligence.

Most platforms stop at reporting events. ROI Engine extends the chain through decision execution and result capture, producing a defensible attribution ledger for revenue impact, risk reduction, and operational efficiency gains.

The Intelligence Loop

From Signal to Measured Outcome

01

Signal Fires

Reflex detects a signal and fires a confidence-scored alert to the appropriate channel.

02

Decision Logged

The ROI Engine captures what action was taken — who responded, what they decided, and when.

03

Outcome Tracked

Business results are measured against the decision — revenue impact, risk avoided, operational change.

04

ROI Attributed

Every signal gets a dollar attribution. You know exactly what your intelligence investment returned.

What the ROI Engine Measures

Full Accountability, Every Layer

Alert Response Rate

What percentage of Reflex alerts were acted on, by whom, and within what timeframe. Identify where signals are falling through the cracks.

Revenue Attribution

Which alerts led to closed deals, expanded accounts, or prevented churn — with dollar values attached to each signal-to-outcome chain.

Risk Avoided

Quantify the business impact of risks that Reflex caught and teams acted on — from compliance issues to infrastructure failures to churn events.

Signal Quality Score

Over time, the ROI Engine scores each signal type against its outcome accuracy — feeding back into Reflex's confidence calibration automatically.

Team Accountability

Who acted on which alerts, how fast, and with what result. Build a culture of intelligence-driven decision making with real accountability attached.

Platform ROI Summary

A board-ready summary of Cal Lattice's total business impact — attributed revenue, risk avoided, and operational savings — updated in real time.
