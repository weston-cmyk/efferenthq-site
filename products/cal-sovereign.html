The Sovereign Guarantee

What "Zero Data Egress" Actually Means

Deployed to Your Environment

Cal Sovereign runs entirely on infrastructure you control — on-prem, private cloud, or air-gapped hardware. We never touch your queries, your answers, or your data.

No External API Calls

Cal's inference happens locally. No calls to OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, or any third-party service. The intelligence is in your building. The model weights are on your hardware.

No Training on Your Data

Your queries and sessions are never used to train or improve any model — not ours, not anyone's. What you ask Cal stays in your environment, period.

Auditable Architecture

We provide full architecture documentation and deployment verification so your security team can confirm zero-egress compliance independently — not just take our word for it.

Full Compliance Support

Documentation packages for HIPAA, FedRAMP, SOC 2, GDPR, and other regulatory frameworks — Cal Sovereign is built to pass the audits you already face.

Updates Without Exposure

Model and platform updates are delivered as signed packages you deploy on your timeline — no automatic connections to EfferentHQ infrastructure required.

Who Cal Sovereign Is For

Healthcare
