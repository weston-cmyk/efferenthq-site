The Full Picture

How the Platform Fits Together

STEP 01

Discovery Maps Your World

The Discovery engine ingests your entire data estate — databases, warehouses, data lakes — and autonomously builds a structured knowledge map. Every table understood, every relationship typed, every schema rationalized. No human configuration required.

STEP 02

Reflex Watches It in Real Time

With the knowledge map in place, Reflex monitors your data continuously. When something important changes — a metric spikes, a pattern emerges, an anomaly appears — Reflex fires a confidence-scored alert to wherever your team works.

STEP 03

ROI Engine Measures What Happens

Every alert Reflex fires is tracked through to its outcome. Was it acted on? What decision was made? What was the business impact? The ROI Engine closes the loop — giving you full accountability over your intelligence investment.

STEP 04

Cal Intelligence Answers Your Questions

Across all of it, Cal's sovereign intelligence layer lets your team ask questions in plain language and get verified, confidence-scored answers drawn from your own data estate plus Cal's global knowledge mesh. One brain. Everything you know.
