Available Specialists

Four Domains. Professional Depth.

Medicine

Clinical protocols, pharmacology, diagnostic criteria, research literature, and regulatory standards — with confidence scores calibrated to medical evidence quality. Built for clinicians, researchers, and health systems who need to trust what they read.

  • Drug interactions and contraindications with citation trails
  • Clinical trial status and efficacy data by indication
  • Diagnostic criteria from current clinical guidelines
  • FDA approval status and post-market surveillance data

$25/mo — Available at Pro tier and above

Law

Statutory law, case precedent, regulatory frameworks, and jurisdiction-specific requirements — with source citations and explicit confidence scoring on legal questions. Not legal advice. Verified legal intelligence.

  • Case law and precedent across major jurisdictions
  • Regulatory requirements by industry and geography
  • Contract clause analysis and risk identification
  • Recent legislative changes and pending regulation

$25/mo — Available at Pro tier and above

Finance

Market structure, regulatory frameworks, financial instruments, accounting standards, and economic research — with confidence scores calibrated to source authority and recency.

  • Regulatory requirements: SEC, FINRA, Basel, MiFID
  • Accounting standards: GAAP, IFRS by industry and situation
  • Financial instrument structures and risk characteristics
  • Economic research and policy impact analysis

$20/mo — Available at Pro tier and above

Climate

Climate science, policy frameworks, emissions data, and sustainability standards — with explicit confidence scoring on projections vs. established findings. For organizations navigating ESG requirements, carbon accounting, and climate risk.

  • IPCC findings vs. current peer-reviewed research
  • ESG reporting frameworks: GRI, TCFD, ISSB, CDP
  • Carbon accounting methodologies and scope definitions
  • Climate risk assessment and scenario analysis

$15/mo — Available at Pro tier and above
