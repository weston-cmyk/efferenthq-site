How Reflex Works

From Data Change to Governed Action

Observe

Reflex watches entities and dependencies from the Discovery map with continuous, governed telemetry windows.

Detect

When shifts appear (anomalies, velocity breaks, pattern emergence), Reflex classifies event type and business relevance.

Score

Signals are confidence-ranked using multi-surface evidence so urgent events escalate while ambiguous events are staged appropriately.

Route

Policy-driven delivery sends alerts to Slack, CRM, email, webhooks, and ops tools with confidence, context, and recommended action framing.

What Reflex Detects

Every Signal That Matters to Your Business

Metric Anomalies

Detects statistically meaningful deviations in revenue, churn, engagement, cost, and operational KPIs with calibrated confidence output.

Velocity Changes

Tracks second-order momentum shifts so acceleration or deceleration surfaces ahead of dashboard lag and weekly reporting cycles.

Risk Patterns

Identifies multi-factor clusters associated with churn, compliance exposure, fraud, or operational failure before threshold breach events.

Data Quality Events

Catches schema drift, null inflation, referential integrity breaks, and distribution shifts before they contaminate downstream intelligence.

Segment Shifts

Custom Signals
