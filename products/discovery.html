How It Works

Autonomous Data Estate Mapping in Four Steps

1

Connect

Point Discovery at your databases, warehouses, or data lakes. It connects via standard interfaces — no agents, no schema changes, no pre-work on your end.

2

Probe

Discovery autonomously probes every table via structured SQL queries — sampling data, analyzing column patterns, inferring types, identifying foreign keys, and detecting semantic relationships that schemas never document.

3

Rationalize

Every table is classified, typed, and placed into a unified knowledge structure. Duplicate concepts across systems are merged. Data lineage is inferred. What was chaos becomes a navigable map.

4

Activate

The knowledge map feeds Reflex (monitoring), Cal Intelligence (Q&A), and your team's tools directly. Every downstream system now operates with a complete, accurate picture of your data estate.

Capabilities

What Discovery Actually Does

Autonomous Schema Discovery

Finds and catalogs every table, view, and stored procedure across all connected systems — without being told what to look for. Including the ones your team forgot existed.

Semantic Column Classification

Goes beyond data types. Discovery infers what a column actually means — distinguishing a "customer_id" from an "order_id" even when they're both integers with no documentation.

Cross-System Relationship Mapping

Identifies relationships between tables across different systems — even when there's no foreign key, no documentation, and no one who knows they're related. Discovery infers it from the data itself.

Data Quality Assessment

Surfaces completeness, consistency, and accuracy issues across your estate as part of the mapping process. You don't just learn what exists — you learn what's reliable.

Continuous Re-Discovery

Data estates aren't static. Discovery runs continuously, detecting schema changes, new tables, and structural shifts — keeping your knowledge map accurate without manual maintenance.

Reflex-Ready Output

Every mapped entity automatically becomes a monitorable surface for Reflex. The knowledge map Discovery builds is the exact input Reflex needs to know what to watch.
